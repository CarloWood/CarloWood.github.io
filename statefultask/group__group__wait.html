void AIStatefulTask::wait ( condition_type  conditions)
protected

Wait for condition.

Go idle if non of the bits of conditions were signalled twice or more since the last call to wait(that_bit). The task will continue whenever signal(condition) is called where conditions & condition != 0.

Parameters
conditionsA bit mask of conditions to wait for.
